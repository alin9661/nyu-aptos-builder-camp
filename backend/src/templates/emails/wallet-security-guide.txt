WHAT IS A CRYPTOCURRENCY WALLET?

A cryptocurrency wallet is like a digital bank account that allows you to:
- Store and manage your digital assets (APT tokens)
- Send and receive transactions on the blockchain
- Interact with smart contracts and applications
- Prove ownership and sign transactions
